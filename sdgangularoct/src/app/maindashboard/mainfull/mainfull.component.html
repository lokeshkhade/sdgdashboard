Shri Vishnu Deo Sai
Hon'ble Chief Minister, C.G

Chhattisgarh is committed to achieve Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in a time-bound manner. The philosophy of 'Antyodaya' coincides with the objectives of SDG. The government is implementing schemes in such a way that the intended benefit of development reaches to the person sitting at the last mile. Achievement of SDG is a strenuous marathon, where success can only be achieved through evidence-based planning, monitoring and implementation with continuous improvement and judicious use of resources.

About Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

  • Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) are adopted by 193 member states including India at the United Nations (UN) summit in September 2015 are collection of 17 global goals aimed at improving the planet and the quality of human life around the world by the year 2030.

  • Chhattisgarh has adopted 106 targets and 275 indicators at State level and 45 targets and 82 indicators for districts.
